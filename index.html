About Lütfi Çağlayan Usta

Hi, I’m Lütfi Çağlayan Usta 🎥👩‍💻 I’m a software engineer passionate about technology, coding, and sharing the career journey. On my YouTube channel, imjenncho, I explore the intersection of lifestyle and tech, sharing insights into software engineering, personal projects, and the importance of maintaining a balanced life. Work-life balance is a strong value of mine, and through my channel, I aim to show that it’s possible to thrive in your career while making time for other important aspects in your life—whether that’s pursuing hobbies, spending time with friends & family, or just taking a moment to recharge. My goal is to provide transparency into the software engineering career path, spark creativity, and empower others to build a fulfilling career and life they’re passionate about. Let’s build, learn, and grow together!
